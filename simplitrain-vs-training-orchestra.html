Frequently asked questions

Everything training teams ask when evaluating SimpliTrain against Training Orchestra.

Which is the best Training Orchestra alternative for corporate training teams?
SimpliTrain is the strongest alternative for teams that want to consolidate ILT scheduling with eLearning delivery, assessments, and content authoring in one platform. Training Orchestra is a specialist scheduling tool that assumes you already have an LMS. SimpliTrain replaces both — and adds a built-in LXP and authoring layer that Training Orchestra cannot provide even with a connected LMS.
How does SimpliTrain's built-in virtual classroom compare to Training Orchestra?
Both platforms support virtual classroom delivery via Zoom and Microsoft Teams integrations. SimpliTrain unifies virtual session scheduling, attendance tracking, and completion records in the same system that handles your eLearning and assessment data — so reporting is consolidated. With Training Orchestra, virtual session data sits in the TMS while learner completion records live in your connected LMS, requiring ongoing data reconciliation between the two.
How can I migrate from Training Orchestra to SimpliTrain?
SimpliTrain's onboarding team supports structured migration from existing TMS and LMS platforms. The typical process covers importing existing course catalogs, trainer profiles, learner records, and historical session data. Because SimpliTrain consolidates TMS and LMS into one system, migration from a two-system stack (Training Orchestra + separate LMS) can often be completed in a single project, reducing transition complexity compared to migrating each system individually.
How does SimpliTrain compare to Training Orchestra on pricing and features?
Pricing: SimpliTrain uses a flat-rate per-admin model with unlimited learners. Training Orchestra offers custom pricing across three tiers (Essential, Professional, Enterprise), with no published rates and no free trial. Features: SimpliTrain covers all core TMS capabilities Training Orchestra offers — scheduling, instructor management, resource allocation, cost tracking — while also providing native LMS, LXP, assessments, and authoring. Training Orchestra is narrower but deeper in scheduling-specific workflows for very high-volume ILT operations.
Does SimpliTrain provide a free native mobile app for learners?
SimpliTrain includes mobile-responsive learning access for all learners. Learners can access course content, join virtual sessions, complete assessments, and view their certificates from any device. Contact the SimpliTrain team for current details on native app availability and platform support.
Can SimpliTrain handle commercial training — selling courses to external clients?
Yes. SimpliTrain includes a full e-commerce course catalog, B2B and corporate sales management, subscription billing, payment handling, and multi-tenant client portals. Commercial training providers can manage the complete cycle from course listing to invoice within the same platform they use for internal L&D operations. This makes SimpliTrain strong for organizations running both internal training programs and fee-based external training businesses simultaneously.
